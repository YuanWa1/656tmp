Description

This screen-space shader analytically generates a transparent sphere with perfect surface normals (N) using implicit equations. By geometrically deriving the secondary intersection at the back of the sphere, it accurately simulates double refraction and surface reflection through a physical medium. The code applies the Fresnel effect at both the entry and exit interfaces to strictly maintain physical energy conservation between transmitted and reflected light, seamlessly blending the final refracted background with a procedural environment map generated by interpolating the Y-axis of the reflection vector.
